pursh iz mira antallis - The latest show achievements   

 

Observing Pursh's health growing up, we decided to show him for the others. And this is how we discovered new and passionate the world of cynology.

We started with the First International Dog Show in Bialystok in 2007. It was the first International Dog Show in the city and the first one in our and Pursh's life. He won the title of The Best Puppy.

We understood that despite of a dog, the person showing it is also important. Showing dogs needs knowledge, good condition, and appropriate preparation. To learn these all we went to Radom for National Dog Show of II, III and VIII Group FCI - Radom 2007.

Pursh won the title of The Best Puppy and was called as absolutely promising. And he won Puppies BIS with the first place.

We have liked Dog Shows. I mean myself and my husband because Pursh is not interested in. We love meeting other bullmastiffs' fans. The breed is not well known, so shows are possibility to see more dogs.
